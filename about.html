Local leadership with deep institutional credibility

Our strength lies in the depth of our leadership and the experience of those who lead every engagement. SIDRA Partners is built on a Saudi-led model, supported by selectively embedded global expertise engaged only where it adds clear and measurable value.

Embedded global expertise (only where value-adding)

Our team comprises partners with decades of senior-level and executive experience across leading companies, institutions, and organizations spanning the Americas, Europe, Middle East, Africa, and Asia-Pacific. Unlike purely Western-centric advisory firms, our advisors have lived and operated on the ground in diverse markets—navigating regulatory environments, building local partnerships, and leading teams across cultural contexts. This real-world, boots-on-the-ground experience in emerging and established markets alike gives us the practical insight to help clients succeed in complex international environments.

Managing Partners and Senior Advisors hands-on in delivery

Our Managing Partners and Senior Advisors remain closely involved throughout delivery, ensuring strategic clarity, execution discipline, and institutional impact.

Experience across policy, infrastructure, and transformation leading global and regional institutions

Our team consists of experienced partners from leading companies, institutions, non-profits, and start-ups worldwide. Their extensive backgrounds have equipped them with the strategic insight and operational expertise necessary to navigate and excel in complex international environments. With deep expertise in strategy and operations, they excel in international settings, especially within infrastructure, energy transition, renewables, resilience and sustainability. Our leaders are known for driving change, managing cross-border teams, and delivering impactful projects that have earned global recognition.

Thought Leadership and Industry Contributions

Our partners are recognized thought leaders who have played pivotal roles in developing taxonomies, regulatory frameworks, and international guidance across critical sectors such as energy, resilient infrastructure, the blue economy, technology, climate, and governance. Their thought leadership is reflected in their active contributions to shaping industry standards and regulatory best practices

Track record with in Investment, Finance and Governance

Our team includes successful investors, finance leaders, policy makers, and CEOs who have guided organizations through various business life cycles and periods of economic volatility.
